Lasagna

Description

One of the easiest to make lasagnas, originating from Italy, very tasty and good for every occasion.

Just mix all the ingredients together and you're good to go.

Ingredients

  • cheese
  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • flour

Steps

  1. Add water to the flour.
  2. Stir for five minutes.
  3. Bake for 20 minutes.
  4. Put the onions and tomatoes on the top.
